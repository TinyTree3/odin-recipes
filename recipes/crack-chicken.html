Crack Chicken

Credit: The Magical Slow Cooker

Description

This recipe uses a slow cooker to make a delicious chicken dish filled with bacon, cheese, ranch, and cream cheese. It's very simple and takes very little effort to make a great side dish along with some rice or bread. Just pop the ingredients in and go about your day and return to some scrumptious Crack Chicken ready to go!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 oz. packet ranch seasoning mix
  • 16 oz. cream cheese
  • 1.5 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 slices cooked bacon
  • 0.5 cup sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Add chicken thighs to slow cooker, making a layer at the bottom.
  2. Sprinkle ranch seasoning mix on top.
  3. Place block of cream cheese on top of chicken.
  4. Cover and cook on LOW for 7 hrs, keeping the lid shut.
  5. Shred chicken with 2 forks.
  6. Stir in cooked cream cheese and bacon.
  7. Spread out into even layer and sprinkle cheese.
  8. Cover for 10 minutes to melt cheese.
  9. Serve over rice and sprinkle green onions over chicken.
  10. Enjoy!
