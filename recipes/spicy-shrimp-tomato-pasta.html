Spicy Shrimp Tomato Pasta

Credit: Budget Bytes

Description

This is a quick and simple recipe to make some delicious pasta with shrimp! It just takes 30 minutes to make this as your lunch or dinner. The ingredient amounts listed will make 4 servings. I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. fettuccine
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tbsp. butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 0.5 lb. peeled shrimp
  • 15 oz. can diced tomatoes
  • 0.25 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes
  • 0.25 tsp. salt
  • Preferred amount of pepper
  • Parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil large pot of water.
  2. Add pasta and continue to boil for 8 mins until tender.
  3. Drain pasta in colander.
  4. While pasta is cooking, add olive oil and butter to large skillet.
  5. Place over medium heat and mince garlic while melting butter.
  6. Rinse shrimp with cool water and drain excess water.
  7. Add shrimp and minced garlic to skillet.
  8. Saute for 5 mins until shrimp turns opaque and slightly pink.
  9. Remove shrimp from skilled and set it aside.
  10. Add can of tomatoes with juices to skillet.
  11. Add crushed red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper to skillet.
  12. Stir and let sauce simmer for 5 mins over medium heat.
  13. When sauce has thickened slightly, add cooked pasta.
  14. Toss to coat in sauce.
  15. Add cooked shrimp pack to skillet and toss to combine.
  16. Chop parsley leaves and sprinkle over top before serving.
  17. Enjoy!
