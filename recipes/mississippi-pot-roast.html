Mississippi Pot Roast

Credit: Belle of the Kitchen

Description

This recipe shows you how to make a yummy pot roast with a chuck roast and a slow cooker. It is incredibly easy to make and very filling too. You just have to dump the ingredients in, leave it alone for a few hours and voila!

Ingredients

  • 3-4 lb. chuck roast
  • 1 oz. packet ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 oz. packet au jus gravy mix
  • 0.25 cup butter
  • 5 pepperoncini peppers

Steps

  1. Place roast in slow cooker.
  2. Sprinkle ranch dressing mix on top.
  3. Sprinkle au jus gravy mix on top.
  4. Place peppers on top of mixes.
  5. Add butter.
  6. Cover and cook on LOW for 8 hrs.
  7. Serve with noodles, rice, or mashed potatoes.
  8. Enjoy!
